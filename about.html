Our story

Built around a simple question

Is everything okay? It's a question we rely on informal systems to answer — until those systems fail. Cover Your Tail exists to fill that gap.

Founder story

Why I built this

I've spent a lot of time in situations where things change quickly.

Where one moment everything feels manageable—

and the next, you're trying to understand what's happening in real time.

You hear possibilities instead of answers.

You watch things unfold without a clear explanation.

And you realize how much of life depends on simply knowing:

Is everything okay?

Outside of those moments, we rely on texts, calls, location sharing—

informal systems that work until they don't.

And when they don't, there's nothing structured to fall back on.

That gap is bigger than we think.

I didn't want to build something that watches people.

I didn't want to build something that reacts after the fact.

I wanted something simple.

Something that fits into real life.

A way to create structure around the moments that matter—

without taking away independence.

That's why I built Cover Your Tail.
JR

Jennifer Robb

Founder & CEO

Our mission

Go where you want. No explanations needed.

You shouldn't have to report your plans to anyone or ask permission to go somewhere alone. Cover Your Tail runs silently in the background — nobody hears a thing unless you don't come back when expected. That's how it should be.
